Lecture — Master's programme, Otto-von-Guericke University Magdeburg

Level: Master Term: Winter Institution: OVGU Magdeburg Language: English

Overview

This course introduces the core methods of econometrics for economics and finance. It covers the classical linear regression model, estimation and inference, and the assumptions behind causal interpretation, building toward the tools used in applied empirical work.

Econometrics (PhD)

Doctoral course, Otto-von-Guericke University Magdeburg / IWH Halle

Level: Doctoral Term: Winter Institution: OVGU Magdeburg / IWH Language: English

Overview

A doctoral-level treatment of the econometric methods used in empirical economics and finance research: identification and causal inference, panel-data and fixed-effects estimation, instrumental variables, and time-series methods, with an emphasis on research design and defensible empirical work.

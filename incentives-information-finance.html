Incentives and Information in Finance

Course, Otto-von-Guericke University Magdeburg

Term: Winter Institution: OVGU Magdeburg Language: English

Overview

This course examines how information asymmetries and incentives shape financial markets and institutions. Topics include adverse selection and moral hazard, contracting and delegated monitoring, signaling and disclosure, and their implications for banking, corporate finance, and financial stability.
